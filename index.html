Research

My long term research interest is the development of AGI systems and their potential to provide answers for life's greatest questions. In the short term for my masters dissertation I hope to investigate how deep learning may be applicaple in areas such as psychophysics to improve our understanding of brain functions such as introspection. An improved understanding of these higher order cognitive functions may symbiotically help to integrate functions such as introspection into future AI models, which I hope may provide valuable steps on the journey towards AGI.
